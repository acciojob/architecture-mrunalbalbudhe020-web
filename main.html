Architectural Design Overview

Unit #1 – Modern Architecture

Architecture blends creativity and engineering to design functional spaces. Modern structures use clean lines, open interiors, and sustainable materials. Architects plan layouts, select materials, and design lighting for both aesthetics and comfort. Good architecture merges beauty, stability, and innovation into every project.
Unit #2 – Structural Design

Successful architectural structures rely on strong foundations and proper planning. Designers consider load distribution, material strength, climate impact, and space requirements. Structural design ensures that buildings remain safe, durable, and efficient. Modern architecture often integrates sustainability, energy-saving concepts, and smart technology.
